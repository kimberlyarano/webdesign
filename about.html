HISTORY

History is the study of past events, people, and societies. It helps us understand how civilizations evolved, how decisions shaped the world, and what lessons we can learn from the past. History is recorded through documents, artifacts, and stories passed down over generations.

History is divided into different periods, regions, and themes, covering everything from ancient civilizations to modern events. It helps us see patterns, understand cultures, and analyze the causes and effects of important moments in time.
